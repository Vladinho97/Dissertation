Study information

This study was self-certified in accordance with School of Informatics Ethics Guidelines and the self-certification was checked by a member of the School's ethics panel.

The data you provide to us will be used for research purposes and for making sure our experiments are working properly. We do not retain or share any information that will allow us or third parties to identify you.

What do we collect?

We record your interactions with our user interface, such as mouse clicks, and demographic information like your gender and age (which you can decline to provide).

In order to keep track of experiment participation, for example to make sure that people don't participate in the same experiment more than once, we record AMT worker IDs. To allow us to troubleshoot potential technical problems, we store information about the browser version you're using, and what country you're visiting our site from.

What do we share?

We may sometimes publish or publicly share anonymous data. This will not include AMT worker IDs or other information that could, in our assessment, allow anyone to identify you.

If you have any questions, please contact Vlad Iordan.


By continuing you agree to the following:

  • You have read the above information
  • You consent to participate in the experiment
  • You are at least 18 years old
  • You understand and agree to the storage, processing and publication of data as described above.
